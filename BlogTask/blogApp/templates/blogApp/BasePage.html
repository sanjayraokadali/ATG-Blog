What is a Blog?

A blog is an online diary or journal located on a website. The content of a blog typically includes text, pictures, videos, animated GIFs and even scans from old physical offline diaries or journals and other hard copy documents. Since a blog can exist merely for personal use, sharing information with an exclusive group or to engage the public, a blog owner can set their blog for private or public access.

“Don’t focus on having a great blog. Focus on producing a blog that’s great for your readers.”

Quote By Brian Clark
Why Blogging?

Write up a few rules and a schedule of events. Set up a blog hop for a certain amount of time. For each of the days, provide a daily prompt. It will pull other bloggers into the fun of writing posts on that topic. Encourage bloggers to visit other bloggers' posts who are also a part of the blog hop.

The first thing you need to decide when you build your blog is what you want to accomplish with it, and what it can do if successful.
